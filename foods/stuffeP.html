Stuffed Peppers

Stuffed Peppers are incredibly delicious and savory. They aren't too difficult to make. That being said, I have personally never made a stuffed pepper. I have eaten them though!

ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • shredded mozzarella
  • rice
  • peppers

instructions

  1. cook the rice and beef seperately. combine them.
  2. split and gut the peppers
  3. put the meat and rice combination into the pepper
  4. sprinkle the cheese on top
  5. throw the peppers at the fucking wall
  6. oven bake 375 for 50 minutes
